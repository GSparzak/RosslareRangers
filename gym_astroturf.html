GYM MEMBERSHIP

Gym membership prices are €30 for 3 months, €60 for 6 months and €100 for the year. €20 deposit for the fob key to gain access. Opening hours are 9am to 9pm seven days a week.

ASTROTURF

Playing football with your friends, club or colleagues on these pitches is a great way to excercise and have fun. All pitches are fully lit so darkness isn't an issue. Also with the rental of a pitch you have use of the changing rooms with showers and ample car parking. Pitches are available throughout the week.

We have pitches available for 5-A-Side, 7-A-Side, 9-A-Side and 11-A-Side. Other clubs also welcome.
